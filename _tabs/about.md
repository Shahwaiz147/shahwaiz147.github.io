# About Me

Hello! I'm **Muhammad Shahwaiz**, a Computer Engineering student at **UET Lahore, Faisalabad Campus**.

I started my university journey in September 2025 with curiosity, excitement, and many challenges. Since then, I have been documenting my experiences, projects, and the lessons I learn throughout my degree. This website serves as my digital portfolio and personal blog, where I share both my academic progress and my life as a university student.

## What I'm Learning

My interests include:

- C#
- Python
- Object-Oriented Programming
- Database Systems (SQL)
- Digital Logic Design
- Machine Learning
- Problem Solving

## My Journey

Living in Ali Hall hostel has taught me independence, discipline, and resilience. From adapting to university life and managing hostel routines to overcoming academic challenges, every experience has helped me grow as both a student and a person.

Rather than focusing only on achievements, I enjoy documenting the complete learning process—the mistakes, the challenges, and the progress that comes with consistent effort.
